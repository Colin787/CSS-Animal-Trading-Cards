The African Lion

The roar of a lion can be heard from 8 kilometers (5.0 miles) away.

  • Common Name: African Lion
  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Weight: 265 to 420 pounds
  • Lifespan: 10-14 Years

Today, there are only half as many African lions than there were 25 years ago.
